# Active Inception Observer Scorecard

Score the interviewer on whether they extracted the hidden constraint rather than
simply completing the conversation.

## Role Discipline

- Interviewer stayed in discovery mode and did not jump straight to solutioning.
- Stakeholder stayed within the provided script and hidden-constraint envelope.
- Observer-scribe captured exact evidence, not paraphrased guesses.

## Extraction Checks

- Hidden NFR or authority boundary was surfaced explicitly.
- Rollback trigger or failure signal was surfaced explicitly.
- The interviewer translated the answer into a durable artifact update.

## Verdict

- Pass if the hidden constraint was extracted and written into the capture artifact.
- Fail if the interviewer accepted a vague answer, skipped the key question, or never
  translated the conversation into a repo artifact.
