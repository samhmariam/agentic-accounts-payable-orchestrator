# NO-AGENT MEMO

## Purpose

Document the scenario where an agent would NOT be the right choice and provide
an alternative recommendation.

## Required Headings

1. Scenario description
2. Rejection criteria applied — which signals are absent or contradicting
3. Recommended alternative approach (rule engine, scripted pipeline, or human workflow)
4. Business framing of the alternative — cost, speed, auditability
5. Conditions under which this decision should be revisited

## Guiding Questions

- Which rejection criterion is strongest for this scenario?
- If someone argues for an agent anyway, what is their best argument and why is it still insufficient?
- What would the compliance team say about the alternative approach?
- Which stakeholder is most likely to push back on the no-agent recommendation, and what evidence would address their concern?

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least two rejection criteria explicitly named and evidenced from the scenario
- Alternative approach is concrete (not "use a simpler system")
- Business framing quantifies at least one dimension (cost, volume, error rate)
- Revisit conditions are specific, not open-ended
- Demonstrates the same rigour as the AGENT_FIT_MEMO (not a dismissive one-liner)
